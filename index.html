CareInbox.ca
Hosted in Canada

Secure clinical document delivery for Canadian healthcare

A dedicated @careinbox.ca address for receiving faxes, referral letters, and clinical documents electronically. No hardware, no paper, no fax machines.

Infrastructure

All data hosted in Canada on GCP infrastructure in Montréal (northamerica-northeast1). TLS encryption enforced on all inbound connections. Documents never leave Canadian jurisdiction.

Security

Documents are ingested into the clinic's EMR system and purged from the inbox after processing. The mailbox is a secure transit layer, not a document store—nothing persists beyond delivery.

For clinics
  1. Each clinic receives a provisioned @careinbox.ca address
  2. Configure your fax service (SRFax, Telus Business Connect, etc.) to forward to your CareInbox address
  3. Documents route directly into your EMR workflow

Designed from the ground up for PIPEDA and PHIPA compliance.
