Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

Operator

CareInbox.ca is operated by Translational AI, located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to Translational AI.

Information We Process

CareInbox receives clinical documents — including faxes, referral letters, and other healthcare correspondence — on behalf of subscribing clinics. These documents are forwarded to each clinic's designated @careinbox.ca address for ingestion into their electronic medical record (EMR) system.

Data Hosting and Infrastructure

All documents are processed through enterprise-grade Canadian-hosted infrastructure provided by Google Cloud Platform (GCP), specifically within the northamerica-northeast1 region located in Montréal, Québec. Documents do not leave Canadian jurisdiction at any point during processing.

Data Retention

Documents are purged from the CareInbox ingestion mailbox after they have been processed and delivered into the clinic's EMR system. The mailbox functions as a secure transit layer, not a persistent document store.

Encryption

  • In transit: TLS encryption is enforced on all inbound and outbound connections.
  • At rest: Documents are encrypted at rest while held in the ingestion pipeline using GCP-managed encryption.

Regulatory Compliance

CareInbox is designed to comply with applicable Canadian privacy legislation, including:

  • PIPEDA — Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (federal)
  • PHIPA — Personal Health Information Protection Act (Ontario)

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, share, or disclose personal health information to third parties except as required to deliver the service (i.e., routing documents to the subscribing clinic's EMR) or as required by law.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, concerns, or access requests, contact us at admin@careinbox.ca.
